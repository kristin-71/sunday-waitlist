Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 October 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 October 2026

Sunday is operated by Kristin Bok, a sole proprietor based in Singapore. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

1. Who we are

Sunday ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application and website operated by Kristin Bok, Singapore. You can contact us at kristin@sundaytheapp.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: your name and email address when you register
  • Pet data: your pet's name, breed, date of birth, and profile photo
  • Health records: vet visits, vaccinations, medications, and notes you choose to log
  • Location data: approximate location when you use the map or Explore features (only when you grant permission)
  • User-generated content: places you pin, photos you upload, and notes you add
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe — we do not store your card details
  • Usage data: how you interact with the app (pages visited, features used) to improve the product
  • Waitlist data: email address if you signed up at sundaytheapp.com before launch

3. Why we collect it

  • To provide and operate the Sunday app and its features
  • To personalise your experience based on your pet's profile
  • To process payments for premium features
  • To send you product updates and announcements (you can opt out at any time)
  • To improve the app through anonymised usage analytics

4. Third-party services

Sunday uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (data stored in Singapore region)
  • Google Maps — map display and place search
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers the Pin It AI feature, which processes content you submit to extract place details. No data is stored by Anthropic beyond the request.
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Formspree — waitlist email collection

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in Singapore. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

6. Your rights under the PDPA

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your data (note: this may affect your ability to use the app)
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at kristin@sundaytheapp.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children

Sunday is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of Sunday after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, contact us at kristin@sundaytheapp.com.
